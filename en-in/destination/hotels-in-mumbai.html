India’s pulsing centre of everything from finance to fashion, Mumbai is where heritage and history sit alongside skyscrapers and hipster havens. Get ready to be swept away by the infectious energy of this island city and its inhabitants.

Taj is the hallmark of world class Indian hospitality across the globe. Our signature 5 star hotels in Mumbai are renowned for splendid experiences, from sophisticated décor, impeccable in-room dining and unique spa treatments to the thoughtful nuances - gourmet treats on perfectly placed pillows and little delights for kids - that put a smile on your face. Our hotels in Mumbai are conveniently located near city’s prominent landmarks like the Mumbai Domestic Airport, Gateway of India & Bandra Bandstand which offer an easy passage to all the corners of the city. Our restaurants in Mumbai serve authentic local, Indian & International cuisines metamorphosing meals into memories at Taj. Checking into Taj – for business or leisure –gently steeps you in all manner of modern comforts, grand architecture, outstanding cuisine, rare art, culture and natural beauty. Be it an important meeting, business conference, or a much awaited get-together, our hotels adapt seamlessly to the nature of event, functioning with incredible efficiency and poise. Taj Hotels in Mumbai are committed to local communities and sustainable practices, which in turn nurtures a global tradition of credibility and trust. We create extraordinary experiences, immersing you in refinement while stepping up to contemporary travel needs and technology, Taj remains timeless.

The Taj Mahal Tower concierge curates a vibrant tour of Mumbai’s native market area of Bhuleshwar, at a 30 minutes drive from the hotel. Besides the 180 years old, perennially bustling Mangaldas and Crawford markets, the tour takes you to the iconic Jama Masjid, Zaveri Bazaar – Mumbai’s premier jewellery market, Phool Galli – the city’s oldest flower market, and Mumbadevi temple, named after and dedicated to the patron goddess of the original archipelago of seven islands that formed the city. The Taj Mahal Tower concierge curates a vibrant tour of Mumbai’s native market area of Bhuleshwar, at a 30 minutes drive from the hotel. Besides the 180 years old, perennially bustling Mangaldas and Crawford markets, the tour takes you to the iconic Jama Masjid, Zaveri Bazaar – Mumbai’s premier jewellery market, Phool Galli – the city’s oldest flower market, and Mumbadevi temple, named after and dedicated to the patron goddess of the original archipelago of seven islands that formed the city. From classic street food to amazing architecture, terrific shopping discoveries and the occasional bovine encounter, this is a delightful snapshot of must-dos in mercurial Mumbai.
